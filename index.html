Privacy Policy

Volume Booster respects your privacy. This extension is designed to work entirely on your device without collecting or sharing any personal data.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No browsing history is tracked
  • No data is stored on external servers
  • No third-party analytics or tracking tools are used

All audio processing happens locally in your browser and only affects the currently active tab when you interact with the extension.
